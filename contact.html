FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can you schedule an inspection?

Most appointments are available within the same week. Call us at (818) 858-0484 for the fastest scheduling — we do our best to accommodate urgent situations.

What if you can't find the leak?

If our team cannot locate the source of your leak, the inspection is completely free. No charge, no questions asked. That's our guarantee.

How long does a pool leak inspection take?

A standard pool inspection typically takes 1–3 hours depending on pool size and the number of potential leak areas. We won't rush — thoroughness is the only way to find every leak.

Do you also do the repairs?

Yes! In addition to leak detection, we offer full repair services including plumbing repairs, skimmer replacement, plaster repairs, and crack repairs. All repairs come with a 90-day warranty.

Is evaporation normal — how do I know if I have a real leak?

Yes, some water loss from evaporation is normal — especially in California's warm climate. A good rule of thumb: if you're losing more than ¼ inch per day, you likely have a leak. You can also do a simple bucket test to check.
