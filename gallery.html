MethodPressure test

Buried Return-Line Leak — Found Without Digging Up the Deck

Homeowner's landscape was staying soggy on the return side of the equipment pad. Hydrophone localised the leak to a 6-ft stretch of 1.5" PVC under the decking. Targeted excavation, replaced the section, closed the deck back up.

MethodHydrophone
