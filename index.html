What We Do

Comprehensive Pool Services

From detection to repair — we handle it all.

Leak Detection

Using hydrophone technology and pressure testing, we locate leaks in pool structure, plumbing, and fittings with pinpoint accuracy.

Plumbing Repairs

We repair and replace damaged underground or above-ground pool plumbing to stop leaks at the source permanently.

Skimmer Replacement

Cracked or leaking skimmers are a common culprit. We replace them correctly to ensure a watertight seal.

Plaster Repairs

We patch and repair cracks and deteriorating plaster in pool shells to prevent water loss and further structural damage.

Crack Repairs

Structural cracks are sealed using professional-grade materials for a long-lasting repair that holds up year after year.

Spa Leak Detection

Spas and hot tubs require specialised inspection. We check jets, plumbing lines, and shell integrity thoroughly.
