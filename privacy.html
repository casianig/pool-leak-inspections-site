Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective date: April 17, 2026  |  Last updated: April 17, 2026

Pool Leak Inspections ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website at poolleakinspections.com or contact us for pool leak detection services, how we use it, and the rights you have over that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information only when you choose to provide it, or when it's automatically generated by standard web activity. Specifically:

Information you give us

  • Contact details — first and last name, email address, and phone number when you fill out our quote or contact form, call us, or email us.
  • Service details — your city, service type (pool/spa/repair), pool age, symptoms, and other project information you share so we can prepare a quote.
  • Messages — anything you write in the "Tell us about your pool" field or send us by email or text.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic log data — IP address, browser type, device type, referring site, and pages visited. This is standard traffic data collected by our hosting provider (Vercel) and is used for security, debugging, and high-level traffic analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your quote request or inquiry and schedule your inspection
  • Contact you about your appointment (call, email, or text)
  • Send follow-up messages related to the service you requested
  • Keep records of work performed, warranties, and estimates
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers or data brokers.

3. SMS / Text Messaging

If you give us your phone number on the contact form or request a callback, you agree that we may contact you by phone call or SMS text message regarding your inspection or quote. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your request. You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP, or by emailing us. Replying HELP will give you more information. Opting out of SMS will not affect other ways we contact you (phone or email).

4. Service Providers

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to run this website and follow up with customers. Each one only receives the information it needs to do its job:

  • Vercel — hosts our website. Receives basic traffic/log data.
  • Formspree — processes contact-form submissions and delivers them to our email.
  • Google Fonts — serves web fonts for typography.
  • Email & phone provider — delivers emails, calls, and text messages to our business line.

These providers have their own privacy policies governing their handling of your data.

5. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use advertising cookies, marketing pixels, or cross-site tracking. Our hosting provider may set basic, essential cookies for site security and functionality.

6. Data Retention

We keep contact form submissions and customer records for as long as needed to service your request, honor warranties, comply with tax and legal recordkeeping requirements, and maintain our business records — typically up to 7 years. You can request earlier deletion (see "Your Rights" below).

7. Your Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA / CPRA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Access a copy of the personal information we have about you
  • Delete your personal information, subject to legal recordkeeping exceptions
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information — though we don't sell or share your information with third parties for advertising
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising any of these rights

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@poolleakinspections.com or call (818) 858-0484. We will respond within 45 days.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adults looking for pool leak detection services. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Security

We use commercially reasonable measures (encrypted web connections, reputable service providers, and access controls) to protect your information. No system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of the page and, where appropriate, notify you.
