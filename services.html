5-Point Pool Leak Analysis

Our comprehensive 5-point leak analysis covers every possible leak source in your swimming pool. We use hydrophone listening technology, infrared cameras, and dye testing to pinpoint leaks in the structure, plumbing, and fittings — without tearing up your yard.

  • Structural shell inspection (cracks, surface damage)
  • Underground plumbing pressure testing

  • Light fixture and step seal inspection
  • Written leak report + repair estimate

Spa & Hot Tub

Spa & Hot Tub Leak Detection

Spas and hot tubs have unique leak points — jets, air channels, plumbing unions, and shell cracks. Our specialists are trained specifically in spa leak detection and will thoroughly inspect every component.

  • Jet fittings and body inspection
  • Plumbing unions and valve check
  • Shell crack and surface inspection
  • Dye testing for pinpoint accuracy

Repairs

Pool Repair Services

Once we find your leak, we fix it. Every repair backed by a 90-day warranty.

Plumbing Repairs & Replacement

Cracked, corroded, or broken underground and above-ground pool plumbing — replaced with high-grade PVC and proper fittings for a leak-free repair that lasts.

✓ 90-Day Warranty

Skimmer Replacement

Skimmers are one of the top sources of pool leaks. We remove the old cracked unit and install a new one with a proper waterproof seal — stopping the leak at the source.

✓ 90-Day Warranty

Plaster Repairs

Deteriorating or cracked plaster lets water seep into the shell. We patch damaged areas with colour-matched, pool-grade material for a seamless, watertight finish.

✓ 90-Day Warranty

Structural Crack Repairs

Cracks in the pool shell worsen over time and cause major water loss. We seal structural cracks with professional-grade materials designed to flex with the pool and last for years.

✓ 90-Day Warranty

Light Fixture Seals

Light niches and conduit penetrations are frequent leak points. We reseal or replace underwater light fixtures and gaskets to stop water intrusion behind the shell.

✓ 90-Day Warranty
