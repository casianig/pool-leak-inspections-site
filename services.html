Repairs

Pool Repair Services

Once we find your leak, we fix it. Our repair services are backed by a 90-day warranty.

Plumbing Repairs & Replacement

We repair and replace cracked, corroded, or broken underground and above-ground pool plumbing. We use high-grade PVC and proper fittings to ensure a long-lasting, leak-free repair.

Skimmer Replacement

Skimmers are one of the most common sources of pool leaks. We remove old, cracked skimmer boxes and install new ones with a proper waterproof seal — stopping the leak at the source.

Plaster Repairs

Deteriorating or cracked pool plaster allows water to seep into the shell. We patch damaged plaster areas with colour-matched materials for a seamless, watertight finish.

Structural Crack Repairs

Cracks in your pool shell can worsen over time and cause significant water loss. We seal structural cracks using professional-grade materials designed to flex with the pool and last for years.

Light Fixture Seals

Pool light niches and conduit penetrations are frequent leak points. We reseal or replace underwater light fixtures and gaskets to prevent water intrusion behind the shell.

Pre-Sale / Pre-Purchase Inspection

Buying or selling a home with a pool? Our comprehensive inspection report gives buyers confidence and helps sellers avoid costly last-minute surprises at closing.

Our Technology

Advanced Tech,
Accurate Results

We don't guess — we use professional-grade equipment to find leaks with precision.

Hydrophone Listening Devices

We can literally hear leaks in underground plumbing without excavation — pinpointing the exact location within inches.

Non-Toxic Dye Testing

Safe, non-toxic dye injected near suspected leak areas confirms the exact crack or gap water is escaping through.

Pressure Testing

We pressure test individual plumbing lines to confirm exactly which pipe is leaking — essential for accurate, cost-effective repairs.
