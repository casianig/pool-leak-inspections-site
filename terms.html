1. Services

Pool Leak Inspections ("we", "us") provides pool and spa leak detection, related diagnostic services, and repair services in Los Angeles and Ventura County, California. A leak inspection typically includes:

  • Pressure testing of pool plumbing lines
  • Hydrophone acoustic listening
  • Visual inspection of skimmers, lights, fittings, and shell
  • Dye testing of suspected leak locations
  • A written report with the location of any leak(s) found and a repair estimate

Repair work is a separate service. We will provide a written estimate before any repair work begins, and will not start repairs without your approval.

2. "If We Can't Find Your Leak, It's Free" Guarantee

Our inspection guarantee means: if our technician completes a full inspection of an accessible, filled, and operational pool or spa and is unable to locate any leak source, there is no charge for the inspection visit. The guarantee applies when the pool is filled to normal operating level, the equipment is running, and our technician has reasonable access to the pool area, equipment pad, and plumbing. The guarantee does not cover situations where the inspection cannot be completed due to factors outside our control (for example, unsafe conditions, empty or drained pool, no water or power, or denied access).
